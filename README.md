# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes the marketing funnel and customer conversion process. It helps identify conversion performance, customer behavior, campaign effectiveness, and key factors influencing successful conversions.

## Objectives

* Analyze the customer journey from lead generation to conversion.
* Measure conversion and drop-off rates.
* Identify high-performing customer segments.
* Evaluate conversion performance by job, education, contact type, and campaign outcomes.
* Support marketing optimization through data-driven insights.

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Leads, Converted Customers, Conversion Rate %, Drop-Off %, Previously Contacted Customers, Non-Converted Customers)
* Marketing Funnel Analysis
* Conversion Rate by Job
* Converted Customers by Month
* Conversion Distribution by Campaign Outcome
* Converted Customers by Education
* Conversion Rate by Contact Method
* Interactive Filters for Detailed Analysis

## Key Insights

1. The marketing campaign generated approximately **41K leads**, resulting in **5K converted customers**.
2. The overall conversion rate is **11.27%**, while the drop-off rate is **88.73%**.
3. Students and retired customers exhibit the highest conversion rates among all job categories.
4. Customers contacted through **cellular communication** show significantly higher conversion rates than telephone contacts.
5. Successful campaign outcomes contribute the majority of conversions, highlighting the importance of effective customer targeting.

## Recommendations

1. Increase marketing focus on customer segments with high conversion rates, such as students and retirees.
2. Prioritize cellular communication channels to improve campaign effectiveness.
3. Analyze reasons for customer drop-off and optimize marketing strategies accordingly.
4. Replicate successful campaign approaches across other customer segments.
5. Monitor monthly conversion trends to identify peak-performing periods and improve campaign planning.
